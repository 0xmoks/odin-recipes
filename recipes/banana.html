Banana Cue

Ingredients:

  • 6 saba bananas (or plantains), peeled
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Bamboo skewers

Steps:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  2. While the oil is heating, roll each banana in brown sugar until fully coated.
  3. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the sugar-coated bananas into the oil.
  4. Fry the bananas, turning occasionally, until the sugar caramelizes and the bananas are golden brown (about 3-5 minutes).
  5. Using tongs, remove the bananas from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  6. Skewer each banana onto a bamboo stick.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy your delicious Banana Cue!
